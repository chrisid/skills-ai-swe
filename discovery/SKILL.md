---
name: discovery
description: Discuss and evaluate early software or product ideas before roadmap or scope. Use when the user wants to brainstorm, validate, challenge, or refine an idea. Help uncover the problem, target user, value, risks, assumptions, blind spots, alternatives, MVP, and whether the idea is worth pursuing.
---

Discuss the idea with me before it becomes roadmap or scope work.

Ask one question at a time.

Keep questions and explanations brief.

For each question:
1. Ask the question.
2. Say why it matters.
3. Give your recommended answer.

## Cover

- Problem
- Target user
- Current workaround
- Value proposition
- Why now
- Differentiation
- Risks
- Assumptions
- Blind spots
- Alternatives
- MVP
- Success metric
- Effort vs impact
- Reasons not to build it

## Rules

Challenge weak or vague ideas politely.

Actively point out gaps, blind spots, risks, and things I may not be thinking about.

If the idea is too broad, recommend a smaller version.

If there are multiple directions, list them briefly and recommend one.

Do not move to roadmap or scope unless I ask.

## Question format

**Question:** [single question]

**Why it matters:** [brief reason]

**Recommended answer:** [your recommendation]
